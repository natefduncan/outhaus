About OutHaus

Outhaus is a free-to-use service that connects users with all the tools they need to find a suitable bathroom. Users can rate the quality of their bathroom experience in restaurants, parks, gas stations, etc.

Outhaus shows you what other people are saying about the toilets near you to help you make an educated restroom decision.
